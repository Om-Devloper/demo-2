Simplify Your Course Payments: Experience A Seamless Payment Process By Scanning The QR Code And Utilizing UPI Or VPA.



Make paying for your desired courses a breeze with our simplified payment process. We offer a convenient and secure way for students to complete their course payments using VPA / UPI and Lookup@ICICI , ensuring a seamless transaction without the need for sharing personal identification.

Here's how it works:

  • Scan the QR code: Easily scan the provided QR code using your preferred scanning app.
  • Enter the payment amount: The QR code will automatically populate the payment amount for your selected course.
  • Confirm the payment: Follow the prompts on your scanning app or banking app to confirm the payment.
  • Authenticate the transaction: Depending on your bank, you may need to enter your UPI PIN or use biometric verification to authenticate the payment.
  • Enrollment secured: Once your payment is successfully processed, your enrollment for the course will be confirmed.

For further information or assistance, please contact our support team. We're here to help you every step of the way.
